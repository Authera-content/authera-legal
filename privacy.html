Authera – Privacy Policy

Last updated: May 2026

1. Overview

Authera ("the Application") is committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what data we collect, how we use it, and how we protect it when you use the Application.

2. Data We Collect

Authera collects only the minimum data necessary to operate. This includes:

  • OAuth tokens – Access and refresh tokens issued by TikTok and Meta when you authenticate. These are stored locally on your device in ~/.authera/ and are never transmitted to Authera servers.
  • API credentials – Keys stored in your local .env.local file to authenticate with AI generation services. These remain on your device at all times.
  • Video metadata – Titles, captions, and scheduling details you enter within the Application. This data is stored locally and shared only with the platforms you explicitly choose to publish to.

3. Data We Do Not Collect

Authera does not collect, store, or transmit:

  • Your name, email address, or personal contact information
  • Payment or financial information
  • Usage analytics or behavioral data
  • Your TikTok or Meta account passwords

4. Third-Party Services

Authera communicates with third-party APIs (TikTok, Meta, AI video providers) solely to perform actions you initiate — such as uploading and scheduling videos. Each third-party service has its own privacy policy governing their data practices.

5. Data Storage & Security

All tokens and credentials are stored locally on your device. Authera does not operate cloud servers or databases. You are responsible for securing your device and local files.

6. Data Retention

You can delete all locally stored Authera data at any time by removing the ~/.authera/ directory from your device.

7. Children's Privacy

Authera is not directed at children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

8. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. The "Last updated" date at the top of this page reflects the most recent revision.

9. Contact

For privacy-related questions, please contact us at: support@authera.app
